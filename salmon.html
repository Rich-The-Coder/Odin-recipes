Salmon

This particual salmon is my favorite recipe to make because not only is it healthy but it also packs a punch for the right indiviaul.
This salmon is to die for and a bang for a buck for a date night or even a calm chill day at home.
Hope you like and please review for future Recipes.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet to medium
  2. Season your Salmon as you would like.
  3. Place Salmon on pan
  4. 2-3 mins for medium well, 5mins on both sides for well done.
  5. Place and plate and squeeze lemon onto salmon.
  6. Serve with any side of choice prefarably rice
