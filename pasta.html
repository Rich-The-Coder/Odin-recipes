Ingredients

  • Ground Turket
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Tomota sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet to medium
  2. Season your Turkey as you would like.
  3. Place turkey on pan
  4. Heat pot with water and some salt
  5. 2 Cups of Rigatoni place in hot water
  6. Serve to family with garlic bread
