Finger Licking good Porkchops

This particual porkchop is so good no napkin is needed!

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet to medium
  2. Season your Pork as you would like.
  3. Place Pork on pan
  4. Cook for 7mins each side
  5. Serve with mash potatoes
